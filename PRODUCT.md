# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Music fans singing along to YouTube videos at home or with friends. They paste a link, want synced lyrics on screen, and may need bilingual display for non-English songs.

## Product Purpose

Client-side karaoke player: YouTube playback plus synced lyrics from LRCLIB and fallbacks. Success means low friction from paste to sing-along, readable lyrics at a glance, and reliable sync controls.

## Brand Personality

Focused, warm, stage-lit. Confident without hype. The UI should feel like a dimmed venue: lyrics are the star, chrome stays out of the way.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS dashboards, cream marketing pages, decorative glass cards, hero metric layouts, and busy landing-page scaffolding. Not a lyrics database admin tool.

## Design Principles

1. Lyrics first: every control serves reading or timing, not decoration.
2. Earned familiarity: standard player patterns (seek bar, play/pause, keyboard shortcuts).
3. State clarity: sync quality, source, and translation status are always legible.
4. Motion with purpose: karaoke highlight and scroll follow the active line; no page-load theater.
5. Works in the dark: default dark stage; light themes remain readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for body and lyric text. Keyboard shortcuts and focus rings on all controls. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for scroll and lyric animations.
